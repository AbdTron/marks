About Us

We are a team of two enthusiastic students from NUML, working together on this project as a fun and creative way to serve our classmates and university community. What started as a small initiative has now evolved into a platform offering a variety of services designed to make student life easier. Our goal is to provide a wide range of helpful services, from academic support to other practical solutions, all aimed at enhancing the student experience. Whether it's marking services, academic assistance, or other future services, we're committed to helping our peers succeed and make their journey through university smoother and more enjoyable
